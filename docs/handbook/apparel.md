# Branded Apparel Issuance

ITAC-branded apparel, including polos, quarter-zip pullovers, and jackets, is purchased with program funds and remains the property of UConn ITAC. Apparel is issued to active team members for professional and everyday use, with the expectation that it be worn during facility assessments, professional events, and other program-related activities where consistent team identification is appropriate.

Apparel is issued on a per-semester or per-year basis at the discretion of the Lead Student and Center Director. Eligibility is based on active participation in the program, including regular attendance at assessments and timely completion of assigned deliverables. Team members who do not meet participation expectations may not be eligible for future apparel issuance.

Because branded apparel is program property, team members are expected to return issued items upon separation from the program or at the end of their issuance period. Items that are not returned may be noted in the team member's program record.

Team members are responsible for maintaining issued apparel in presentable condition for professional use. Lost or damaged items should be reported to the Lead Student. Replacement items are subject to availability and program budget.
